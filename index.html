Information About Helyx

The Helyx Initiative was brought to Burlington High School to help create a welcoming community for students interested in STEM. We plan on promoting STEM education and careers through a multitude of different events. We believe our school doesn't have enough education and drive towards careers in STEM. We wanted to fill the gap and expose our school to STEM education, professions, and opportunities.
